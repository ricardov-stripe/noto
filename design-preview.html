01 · Typography — the heart of it

Five families, organized by surface

The product has two surfaces. Canvas is what you wrote — set in iA Writer DNA. Chrome is what the app shows around you — set in Geist + Cabinet. The two never blur. Inline `code` inside notes is iA Writer Mono (sibling to Quattro). Status pills, due dates, shortcuts in the chrome are Geist Mono. They live on different surfaces by rule.

02 · Color

Warm paper · grounded terracotta

Restrained, warm-neutral, single grounded accent. The accent does the AI-signaling job in earth tones — no purple, no violet, no blue-purple gradients anywhere in the product.
